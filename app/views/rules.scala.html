Rules

• This game can be played with 1-4 Players.
• Each player has four pieces to move, and they all start at their home field.
• A piece can be moved on their own starting point if a player rolls a six.
• If all pieces are still in their home field, a player is allowed to roll the dice three times.
• If a six is rolled, the player is allowed to roll the dice again.
• When a piece is already on the starting point and a six is rolled, the player first has to move the piece that is on the starting point.
• The goal is to get all the four pieces into their finish fields.
• Pieces can jump over other pieces and throw out pieces from other players if they land on their field.
• A Player cannot throw out his own pieces and cannot advance further than the last field in the home row.
